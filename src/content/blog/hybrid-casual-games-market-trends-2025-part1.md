## Introduction

Q2 2025 marks a turning point for hybrid casual games with revenue doubling YoY among the top 10 titles. Puzzle & arcade dominate, while action lags behind.

Leaders like **Color Block Jam**, **Screwdom**, **All in Hole**, and [Hole People](https://www.holepeoplelevel.com) showcase distinct monetization playbooks.

For developers, investors, and product teams, the lesson is clear:  
**The future of casual gaming lies in accessible mechanics combined with sustainable monetization and live-ops.**

## Key Questions Answered

### Q1: How are hybrid casual games different from hyper-casual?

- Hybrid casual blends lightweight mechanics with deeper IAP systems  
- Revenue no longer relies solely on ads  
- Battle passes, ad-removal, and seasonal events drive LTV
