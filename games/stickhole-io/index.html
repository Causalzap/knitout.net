Game Overview

How to Play Stickhole.io?

  • Controls: Use WASD keys (Player 1) or arrow keys (Player 2) to move the hole around the map.
  • Goal: Swallow stickmen and objects to increase the hole size and defeat opponents.
  • Single-player: Collect enough units to challenge and defeat the boss.
  • Two-player: Out-collect your opponent before time runs out.
  • Strategy: Avoid obstacles and plan paths strategically for maximum collection.
  • Customization: Collect coins and gems to unlock upgrades and new skins.
