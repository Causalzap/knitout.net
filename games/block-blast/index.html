How to Play Block Blast?

  • Goal: Arrange blocks of the same color to form complete horizontal lines or vertical columns to eliminate them from the board.
  • Pure Strategy: This game strips away the chaos of gravity to focus entirely on calculated placement.
  • Logical Requirement: Elimination is achieved only when the small squares are completely filled in the row or column—forcing players to be precise and strategic with every placement.
  • Three-Block System: The blocks ready for use are displayed at the bottom of the screen—always showing three randomly generated blocks. You must calculate the optimal placement for all three before placing the first one.
  • Score Maximization: Every successful column or row destroyed grants you additional points. Balance clearing space with achieving multi-line destruction for the highest score potential.

What's the main difference between Block Blast and Tetris?

Is there a time limit in Block Blast?
